## Example itinerary

- 8:30 AM - Trailhead
- 8:45 AM - Skinning up (7 miles, 3,600 ft gain, ~4 hours)
- 1:00 PM - Summit
- 1:30 PM - Ski back down to flat road (4.5 miles, 3,600 ft descent, ~1.5 hours)
- 3:00 PM - Put skins back on
- 3:15 PM - Skin out flat road (2 miles of flat, ~1 hour)
- 4:15 PM - Back at car
