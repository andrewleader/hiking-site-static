---
title: "Hex Mountain Backcountry Skiing Dec 2021"
---

Super fun day! Really mellow route, hardly steep enough in some spots, but mostly all open skiing, no dense trees!

- 
- 

When there's uphill sections, do not try to cut those, it's easiest to just go up and then down. The ridge is so curved and mellow that it's impossible to "traverse" back to it.
