## Pitch 1 - 5.5, ~30m

Shortest and technically most difficult pitch. Climb the small slab/flake (crux). Then easy climbing up to a belay beneath the whitish south corner that splits the arete.

## Pitch 2 - Chimney, ~60m

Scramble climber's right into the gully and climb the gully until it becomes a chimney (easy 5th). Then follow easier ground, broken rock, and ledges, and then up along the crest.
