## Approach

GPS map here: [https://caltopo.com/m/KUCA](https://caltopo.com/m/KUCA)

Park at the [Lower Town Wall](https://goo.gl/maps/GWri3ZdpzG14zPKg9), approach to the [Country Area](https://www.mountainproject.com/area/105790671/the-country-area) (hike up from the parking lot and within one minute when crossing the railroad, continue right along the railroad for two minutes till the trail/road branches off to the left and follow that... in one more minute you'll find the big wide area with the closed in mining tunnel).

From the Country Area by the closed in mining tunnel, continue past the sign and then immediately take a left turn through blackberry.

Walk at most 1 more minute... if you start going uphill you've gone too far. Turn around and look for a tree near the wall... it has rungs in the back of it! That's the start!
