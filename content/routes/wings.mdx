Doesn't really sound that neat, only a 200 ft cliff? Not great rock quality either? Apparently it is **sport**. Maybe bring a single rack and nuts just in case?

## Approach

The road is essentially right below the peak (200 vertical feet below and 0.1 mile away). It looks like you park a little before you get directly underneath, and then maybe there's some traverse trails on the steep dirt. Looks like 0.2 miles, 200 ft gain, 20 minutes or less.

## Climb

This is an interesting route ascending the northernmost crack on the W face. Climb a left facing corner along a pillar to a small belay stance atop the pillar. Continue ascending right on some loose rock and follow a shallow chimney to the *terrace*.
