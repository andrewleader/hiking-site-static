A newer climb, more accurately rated (not sandbagged).

## Gear

- Most people seem to bring **double 0.1's** for the crux P3 5.10+ pitch

## P1 - 5.10c

Clip a couple bolts then head left into a layback flake. Climb this until it seems like it's going to disappear, then head right over a small roof, where you will find another bolt.

## P2

Move over easy slab with bolts.

## P3 - 5.10+

The crux. Move left from the belay up a steep arete into a corner. Move right out of the corner over steep ground to clip a couple bolts. Then head right again and up. Finish back to the left over steep jugs to a chain anchor.

**Gear** for P3

- "At least 2 blue aliens, would have placed 3-4 if I could"
- "I did use two 00 (0.1 cam) and 0 (0.2 cam) metolius" (solid 5.10 climber)
- "I had a single Red C3 #0.1"
- "single set of C3's" (0.0-0.3?)
- "Double 0.1-0.2"
- Blue alien == 0.375" (seems equivalent to 0.1)
- 0.1 Cam == 0.34-0.54 in
- 00 metolius == 0.34 - 0.47" (equivalent to BD 0.1)
- 0 metolius == 0.39 - 0.59" (possibly similar to 0.2)
