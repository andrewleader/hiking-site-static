Descent

The final top ridge looks way too steep and dangerous especially in higher avy conditions, so skipping the very top

## Example itinerary

- 8:30 AM - Trailhead
- 8:45 AM - Skinning up (3.7 miles, 3,400 ft gain, ~4 hours)
- 12:45 PM - Summit (well, technically below the summit)
- 1:15 PM - Ski down (3 miles, 3,400 ft descent, ~2 hours)
- 3:15 PM - At car
