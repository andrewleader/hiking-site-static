Crappy weather and rocks falling every 2 minutes... no thanks, turnaround it was.

Map: [Observation Rock - CalTopo](https://caltopo.com/m/HS4E5)

**Key learnings**

- Drive to trailhead is 20 mins shorter than Google says (1:50 rather than 2:10).
- Walkie talkies could be useful if windy &amp; foggy (since might not be able to hear or see each other while climbing)
