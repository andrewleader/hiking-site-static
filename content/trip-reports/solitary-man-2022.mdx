After climbing Condor, we wanted to try out Solitary Man as a group of 3 with Josh and Alexis.

The route is NOT bolted for rappels (you're expected to rappel the other route, or walk off the gully). The route just has hangers, no rings/chains.

## P1 - 5.10-, 30m, ~9 bolts

Our info claimed this was 5.9, 35 meters, but it definitely felt 5.10-, and it definitely was just under or exactly at 30 meters. Pretty sustained at 5.9/5.10- for a number of moves, very slabby.

Our info claimed this was 5.10-, 30m, but it definitely felt like 5.10 slab at one crux move, and was definitely only 27m. Pretty sustained pitch.
