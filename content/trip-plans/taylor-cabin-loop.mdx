## Itinerary

### Day 1

3:00 PM - hike 8.0 miles

Arrive at FR 525C, hike from Sycamore Pass down to Taylor Cabin

6:30 PM

Arrive at Taylor Cabin, camp

### Day 2

8:00 AM - hike 2.0 miles

Leave Taylor Cabin heading NE to Taylor Cabin Trail

9:00 AM - hike 7.0 miles

Hike up Taylor Cabin Trail to the rim

1:30 PM - hike 7.0 miles

Hike along Casner Mountain back to car

5:30 PM

Back at car
