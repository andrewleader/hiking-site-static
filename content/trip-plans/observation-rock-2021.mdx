## Itinerary

- 3:50 AM - Leave home (2:10 hr drive)
- 6:00 AM - Arrive
- 6:15 AM - Start hiking
- 10:00 AM - At base (5 miles, 3,750 ft gain, take more direct way in, all snow free till climb itself basically)
- 10:30 AM - Climbing
- 1:30 PM - Top
- 1:45 PM - Hike off
- 2:15 PM - Back at base, hike back (5 miles, mostly downhill, 3 hours)
- 5:15 PM - Back at car
- 5:30 PM - Drive home (2:20 hr drive)
- 7:50 PM - Home
