## Todra gorge multi-pitch

[https://www.climbing-in-morocco.com/Guidebook.G.htm](https://www.climbing-in-morocco.com/Guidebook.G.htm)

- [Albert ](http://hiking.andrewleader.net/routes/albert/)- 60m rope fine, 11 quickdraws or ~16 if linking
- [Qui L'eut cru?](https://www.mountainproject.com/route/114043063/qui-leut-cru) - 60m sounds fine, 12 quickdraws
- [Une arête dans la gorge](https://www.mountainproject.com/route/112140784/une-arete-dans-la-gorge) - Might need to do a 40m rappel? Or maybe could walk far around?
- [Hannah chez les grands](https://www.mountainproject.com/route/112178059/hannah-chez-les-grands) - 60m sounds fine, walk off, 12 quickdraws
- Berbertraum - 60m, 13 draws, 14 pitches, walk off, 5c, within Mansour gauche

## Todra gorge single-pitch

- Secret Garden crag - Mostly 70m
- Sahara - 2/3rds 60m
- Dromadaire - Half 70m
- Mansour gauche - Half 70m
- Mansour gauche G - Half 70m
- Mansour gauche D - Mostly 70m
- Mansour droite - Half 70m
- Jardin des rockes - Mostly 60m
