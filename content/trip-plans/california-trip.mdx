### Deep Creek Hot Springs

![](http://studentreader.com/files/deep-creek-hot-springs/deep-creek-hot-springs-3074-full.JPG)

We'll be taking the Bradford Ridge Trail to Deep Creek Hot Springs, which is&nbsp;**2.5 miles**&nbsp;and&nbsp;**800 feet of elevation change**.

#### Resources

- Map ([CalTopo Online](http://caltopo.com/map?id=6T01))
- [Google Maps Trailhead/Parking](https://www.google.com/maps/place/34%C2%B019'05.5%22N+117%C2%B011'45.2%22W/@34.3183356,-117.1955095,376m/data=!3m1!1e3!4m2!3m1!1s0x0:0x0)&nbsp;(34.3182, -117.1959)
- [Motor Vehicle Use Map (look at North Shore)](http://www.fs.usda.gov/Internet/FSE_DOCUMENTS/stelprdb5202570.pdf)

#### Bradford Ridge Trail

This trail starts south of Deep Creek Hot Springs. This trail doesn't involve any dirt roads. Park by the bridge on Highway 173 at&nbsp;[34.3182, -117.1959](https://www.google.com/maps/place/34%C2%B019'05.5%22N+117%C2%B011'45.2%22W/@34.3183356,-117.1955095,376m/data=!3m1!1e3!4m2!3m1!1s0x0:0x0).
