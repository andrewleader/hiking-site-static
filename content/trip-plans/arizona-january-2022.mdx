## Sedona classics

- [**Regular Route**](http://hiking.andrewleader.net/routes/regular-route-2/) - ⭐⭐ 5.7 T, 3 pitches (40 min approach)
  - Mellow climb, but it's a tower climb so potentially worth doing
- Original North Face Route - ⭐ 5.7+ T, 3 pitches (30 min approach)
  - Also a tower climb, Regular Route looks more fun though
- [**Dr. Rubo's Wild Ride**](http://hiking.andrewleader.net/routes/dr-rubos-wild-ride/) - ⭐⭐⭐ 5.9 T, 4 pitches (30 min approach)
- [Sedona's Scenic Cruise](http://hiking.andrewleader.net/routes/sedonas-scenic-cruise/) - ⭐ 5.9 T, 5 pitches (the traverse one, and 1hr approach+double ropes)
- Mars Attacks - ???? 5.9 PG13, hugeee long traverse
- [**Nirvana**](http://hiking.andrewleader.net/routes/nirvana/) - ⭐⭐ 5.9 T, 3 pitches (40 min approach)
  - Seems potentially fun, nothing super special
- Original Route - ???? 5.9+ T, 5 pitches (has the giant jump across for rappelling)
- North Face/West Crack - ???? 5.9+ (has ANOTHER jump, this time on route and mandatory)
- Earth Angel - 5.10- T, 6 pitches (same tower as Touched By An Angel)
  - Maybe need #0.0-0.1
- The Windows Route - 5.10 T, 3 pitches (has the wide chimney between two pillars)
- [Touched By An Angel](http://hiking.andrewleader.net/routes/touched-by-an-angel/) - ⭐⭐ 5.10 T, 5 pitches
  - 45 min approach, single 70m or double 60's, maybe need #0.0-0.1
    - Has the splitter pitch, but it is somewhat fist crack size, and somewhat traversy
- **[Coyote Tower](http://hiking.andrewleader.net/routes/coyote-tower/)** - ⭐⭐⭐ 5.10c T, 6 pitches
  - 25 min? **Double 60m ropes**, looks like good quality climbing!
- **Cragging at [Waterfall ](https://www.mountainproject.com/area/106461132/the-waterfall)in Oak Creek Canyon**
  - Lots of 1-pitch 5.10+ basalt cracks, 30-40 min approach

## Queen Creek Canyon climbing

- [Atlantis S Side](https://www.mountainproject.com/area/113914004/atlantis-south-side), 5 min approach (have to [sign online waver](http://queencreekclimbing.com/) to get access), enough for 1 full day!
  - 3-pitch 5.9 [The Trident](https://www.mountainproject.com/route/113675537/the-trident)
    - And about 8 other single-pitch climbs
    - Maybe could do a few on the north side too but there's not as many
    - There's a few in Funny Farm next door too
- [The Pond](https://www.mountainproject.com/area/105788092/the-pond) 15 min approach (have to sign waver again), enough for 1 full day!
  - At least 14 single pitch routes that look good!
- Could probably do Hackberry Creek if we want a third day, it has a few decent looking ones
