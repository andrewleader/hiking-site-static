## Itinerary

Dusk at 5:00 AM, sunrise at 5:37 AM, sunset at 8:51 PM

- 3:30 AM - Leave home (1:30 hr drive)
- 5:00 AM - Arrive
- 5:15 AM - Hiking (~6 hours, 5.5 miles, 4,600 ft gain)
- 11:15 AM - Summit
- 11:30 AM - Hike back (~4 hours)
- 3:30 PM - Car
- 5:00 PM - Home

## Weather

WARM day, full sun. Temps starting at high 50's when we start hiking, then low 60's and ending day at high 60's.

@3,700 ft (700 above start), Sunny, with a high near 68. Light and variable wind becoming west 5 to 9 mph in the morning.
