## Itinerary

**Thursday**

- 2:10 AM - Drive to Paradise (2:30 hr drive)
- 4:40 AM - At Paradise
- 5:00 AM - Hiking (6 hr hike)
- 11:00 AM - At Camp Muir (12 hrs acclimatization)
- 11:00 PM - Start climbing (4,500 ft, est. 9 hours, starting at 10,050 ft)

**Friday**

- 4:00 AM - Top of Cleaver (12,250 ft, no earlier than 4, want some light after cleaver, so that's 2,200 ft gain, 4:20 hours)
- 8:00 AM - Summit, descend (descending should take half the time it took to ascend, so 4.5 hours)
- 11:00 AM - Bottom of cleaver
- 12:30 AM - Muir
- 12:30 PM - Hike back down
- 3:30 PM - At car
- 6:30 PM - Home
