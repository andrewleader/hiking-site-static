## Itinerary

**Saturday**

- 11:00 AM - Drive to [The Park Side Cafe](https://g.page/olyparksidecafe?share), get lunch
- 12:30 PM - Lunch at Park Side Cafe
- 1:30 PM - Arrive [at trailhead](https://goo.gl/maps/e7C6PfRM6Gp3pXeW7)
- 2:00 PM - Backpack in to climbers camp (5.5 miles, 2,500 ft gain, ~3 hours)
- 5:00 PM - Arrive at camp, set up camp (sunset at 7:14 PM)
  - There should be a small stream next to camp to filter water, on Sept 12th it still had enough water to filter

![The climbers camp](https://andrewbares.blob.core.windows.net/hiking-blog-images/2022/09/Camp.YbGaDtNw.jpg)

**Sunday**

- 6:30 AM - Start getting up (sunrise at 6:54 AM)
- 7:00 AM - Hike to summit (1.7 miles, 3,900 ft gain, ~3.5 hours)
- 10:30 AM - Summit
- 11:00 AM - Hike down (1.7 miles, 3,900 ft loss, ~2.5 hours)
- 1:30 PM - Back at camp
- 2:30 PM - Backpacking out (5.5 miles, ~2.5 hours)
- 5:00 PM - Back at car (2:30 hr drive)
- 7:30 PM - Back home

## Gear

- Clothes for overnight low of 49 degrees, hiking temps should be mild (58 degrees, or briefly 49 degrees but then should have direct sun)
- Sunglasses/sunscreen
- No snow gear needed (route snow free)
- Water filter
- 1L water for the initial backpack in
