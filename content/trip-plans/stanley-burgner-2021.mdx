Sunday

- 10:30 AM - At start
- 11:00 PM - Climbing (6 pitches, ~30 min/pitch)
- 2:00 PM - Top of climb
- 2:30 PM - Rappel (5 rappels, ~10 min/rappel, ~1hr)
- 3:30 PM - Base
- 4:00 PM - Back at saddle/gear
- 4:30 PM - Hike back down

## Weather

**Summary:** Full sun but chilly. Starting off and hiking in, temps will be consistently 46 degrees or even a bit cooler as we get up there, like 44 degrees. Winds will be moderate starting at 7mph and increasing to 10-12 mph. During the climb, it'll be about 43 degrees, but hopefully the route will be full sun and hopefully the ridge will block the wind.
