- Passport or whatnot for border
- Food
  - 1 breakfast (Sunday morning driving to hot springs)
    - 1 lunch (Sunday at the hot springs)
    - 1 dinner (Sat night at Joffre Lakes)
- Hiking stuff
  - Hiking shoes (can be running shoes or actual boots) (will be walking through snow)
    - 2 liters of water bottles
    - Light jacket for 45 degree weather
    - Rain jacket for rain :(
    - Swimming suit for hot spring!
    - Towel for hot spring
    - Sunglasses (snow is BRIGHT)
    - Sunscreen
    - Backpack to carry all of that
- Change of clothes - Socks/shirt/pants/underwear
- Toothbrush/overnight essentials

### The drive there

6 hours 30 minutes total

- Bellevue to Vancouver: 2 hrs 40 mins
- Bellevue to Whistler: 4 hrs
- Bellevue to Pemberton: 4 hrs 35 mins
- Vancouver to Whistler: 1 hr 35 mins
- Vancouver to Pemberton: 2 hrs 5 mins
- Pemberton to Hot Springs: 2 hours

### The hike

![](http://whistlerhiatus.com/images/stories/Driving/Keyhole/lillooet%20river%20trail%20map%20to%20keyhole%20hot%20springs.jpg)

- 1.3 mile one-way hike to the spring
- Will take 1 hour to hike there
- Likely will be some snow on the trail
- Shouldn't need snowshoes or spikes, just normal hiking shoes
