Base to summit and back in a day!

### Resources

- Map ([CalTopo Online](http://caltopo.com/map?id=1577))

### Trails

- **Ventana Canyon Trailhead** - 19.6 miles, 9,000 feet gain
- **Esperero Canyon Trailhead** - 15.7 miles, 7,500 feet gain
- **Romero Canyon Trailhead** - 13.0 miles, 6,300 feet gain

## Itinerary

#### 6:00 AM - hike 15.7 miles

Start at Esperero Canyon Trailhead

#### 12:40 PM - hike 19.6 miles

At summit, hike back to Ventana Canyon Trailhead

#### 6:00 PM

Back at Ventana Canyon Trailhead
