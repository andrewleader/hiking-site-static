Remote 4th class climb

### Info

For general info, see [Bonanza Peak - Mary Green Glacier](http://hiking.andrewbares.net/hike.php?id=5)

### Itinerary

#### Friday, July 6th

- 4:45 AM - Drive over (3 hour drive)
- 8:00 AM - At boat dock (30 mins early recommended)
- 8:30 AM - Ferry departs
- 11:45 AM - Arrive in Lucerne (port of Holden)
- 2:45 PM - Take bus to Holden
- 3:30 PM - Arrive in Holden Village
- 3:45 PM - Hike to camp
- 6:45 PM - Arrive at camp (3 hours, 3,100 ft gain)

#### Saturday, July 7th

- 2:00 AM - Start hiking
- 8:00 AM - Summitted Bonanza (5-6 hours)
- 8:30 AM - Descend back down to camp (5 hours)
- 1:30 PM - Back at camp
- 2:00 PM - Start hiking to Martin (3 hours)
- 5:00 PM - Summitted Martin
- 5:30 PM - Descend back down to camp (3 hours)
- 8:30 PM - Back at camp

#### Sunday, July 8th

- 8:00 AM - Pack up camp
- 9:00 AM - Descend down to Holden (2 hours, 3,100 ft loss)
- 11:00 AM - Arrive for bus
- 11:15 AM - Bus down to ferry
- 12:00 PM - At ferry dock
- 12:20 PM - Ferry departs
- 2:45 PM - Arrive in Chelan
- 3:00 PM - Drive back to Bellevue (3 hour drive)
- 6:00 PM - Back in Bellevue
