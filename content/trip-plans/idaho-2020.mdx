## Day 4

### Scrambling option - Redfish Peak

Could also possibly go to Goat Perch, apparently there's a class 5 scramble there, could maybe combine both.

![](https://andrewbares.blob.core.windows.net/hiking-blog-images/2020/05/image.pggeiKBZ-1024x534.png)

### Climbing option - Four Horsemen or Goat Perch

[Four Horsemen is 5.9, 14 pitches](https://www.mountainproject.com/route/108308861/four-horsemen), and apparently some loose blocks that you have to "set the belay up out of the line of fire".

![](https://cdn-files.apstatic.com/climb/108308933_large_1494275267.jpg)

[Goat Perch is 5.9, 6 pitches](https://www.summitpost.org/north-ridge/168160). Not on Mountain Project, but sounds like maybe better climbing? Rappel uses two ropes. Can't find much info on it other than the summitpost description.

![](https://andrewbares.blob.core.windows.net/hiking-blog-images/2020/05/1443181.y1D3QIQ.jpg)
